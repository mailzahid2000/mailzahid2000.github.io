What We Offer

Care shaped around
every young person

Six specialist services — all built around what each individual genuinely needs to feel safe, grow, and thrive.

01 — Residential Care

A home, not a placement

Safe, carefully staffed environments with 24/7 support. Small homes of 2–4 young people ensuring genuine individual attention and real belonging.

02 — EBD Support

Emotional & behavioural expertise

Specialist support for young people with Emotional and Behavioural Difficulties using trauma-informed, relationship-based practice every day.

03 — Education

Learning never stops

Close liaison with schools, tutors, and educational professionals ensures every young person's academic journey stays on track throughout placement.

04 — Life Skills

Built for independence

Real independence embedded in daily life — cooking, budgeting, communication, and self-advocacy through genuine lived experience at home.

05 — Semi-Independent Living

The bridge to adulthood

Supported living pathways for 16–21 year olds — a carefully managed transition from residential care into confident, independent adult life.

06 — Emergency Placements

Ready when it matters most

Rapid-response capability for young people needing immediate safe housing. We are equipped and available for urgent referrals at any hour.

Our Approach

How a placement works

Clear, caring, and collaborative — from first contact to settled placement.

1

Initial Referral

Contact us directly or via frameworks. We respond to all enquiries the same day, including emergencies.

2

Needs Assessment

We review background, needs, and care plans to confirm a strong match before placement begins.

3

Personalised Plan

A tailored support plan built collaboratively with the placing authority, family, and young person.

4

Settled & Supported

Consistent, structured care with regular reviews and transparent reporting for placing teams.
